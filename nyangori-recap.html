Nyang'ori Youth Camp

Experience the powerful messages and life-changing moments from our recent outreach event at Nyang'ori. Below are highlights from the exceptional speakers who blessed the event.

John Begisen

"End-Time Prophecy"

John Begisen delivered an inspiring message on end-time prophecy giving highlights of Christ's imminent return, calling for repentance, steadfast faith, and spiritual readiness while interpreting biblical signs to warn of coming tribulation and final judgment.

Bishop Beatrice Mbaya

"Encouragement To The Youth"

Bishop Beatrice Mbaya emphasized encouraging youth to grow spiritually, develop their God-given potential, and embrace leadership roles within the community as faithful stewards and examples of Christ's love and service.

Benjamin Wanyama

"Spiritual Growth & Spiritual Cities"

Benjamin Wanyama focused on how believers develop their faith while building spiritual communities that reflect God's kingdom principles, transforming both individual souls and collective environments through the love of Christ.

Joseph Wanyama

"The Act of Forgiveness"

Joseph Wanyama highlights on forgiveness as a divine commandment, spiritual healing process and path to personal liberation that reflects God's mercy toward humanity.

Event Recap

The Nyang'ori Gospel Outreach Event was a resounding success, bringing together over 500 attendees from various communities. The event featured powerful worship sessions and inspiring messages from our distinguished speakers

We witnessed numerous testimonies of transformation and renewed hope among attendees. The success of this event has laid a strong foundation for future outreach programs in the Nyang'ori region.
