Nyang'ori Youth Camp

This was a powerful and transformative experience for youths from various church denominations across Western Kenya. They gathered under the anointed worship of the Umoja Voices Band from Serem and were deeply impacted by messages from various servants of God. The outreach event, which was originally scheduled to take place at the Pentecostal Bible College in Nyang’ori, was relocated to the Grand Royal Hotel—thanks to the hotel's significantly discounted rates and convenient conference facilities. Below are highlights from the exceptional speakers who ministered during the event.

Evangelist John Begisen

"End-Time Prophecy"

Evangelist John Begisen delivered an inspiring message about current world events and the signs foretold by God’s prophets in the Bible, including Jesus Christ’s own discourse on the Mount of Olives. These signs clearly indicate that we are living in the final days of life on this planet earth.

Therefore, we must be aware of Christ’s imminent return and ensure that we are born again, living lives marked by repentance, steadfast faith, and spiritual holiness and readiness. We are called to interpret biblical signs to warn others about the coming rapture of the Church into heaven, the tribulation that will follow for those left behind, and the final judgment—where the saved will dwell forever in the presence of God, while the wicked will be cast into the eternal lake of fire.

Bishop Beatrice Mbaya

"Encouragement To The Youth"

With a mother’s heart and deep spiritual wisdom, Bishop Beatrice Mbaya saw clearly into the struggles our youth face today. She boldly named the roots of their pain and illuminated the way forward—through the power of faith in Jesus Christ. She passionately called on the youth to boldly rise up in Christ, grow in their walk with God, unlock their divine potential, and lead by example—just as Christ did. Her message was a heartfelt charge: to step into leadership with courage, love, and service, becoming shining lights of God’s grace in their communities.

In all these Jesus must be the Lord of their lives.

Youth Pastor Benjamin Wanyama

"Spiritual Growth & Spiritual Cities"

With the heart of a young man and the fear of God, Pastor Benjamin Wanyama spoke powerfully into the struggles facing today’s youth. He dug deep into the reasons behind their spirit of rebellion—the belief that they know better than their elders—and courageously exposed the dark forces of satanic worship and drug addiction that are silently creeping into our schools. With love and urgency, he declared the only true answer: surrendering completely to Christ Jesus.

Pastor Benjamin passionately shared how the Holy Spirit offers victory, strength, and spiritual gifts to every young person willing to stand firm in their faith. He warned that the alluring traps of this world are dragging many into depression, broken dreams, and even suicide. But hope remains! He urged the youth to rise up, to deepen their trust in God, and to become shining lights to those around them—living testimonies of God’s kingdom, transforming hearts and communities through the unstoppable love of Christ.

Joseph Wanyama

"The Act of Forgiveness"

Evangelist Joseph Wanyama underscored the vital foundations of life in Christ: love, forgiveness, holiness, and purity. Forgiveness, as commanded by Jesus Christ, is not only essential for anyone aspiring to follow Him, but also a prerequisite for receiving God's own forgiveness and redemption. As the youth embrace these divine commands—to forgive without reservation, to love one another deeply, and to live in holiness, set apart from the ways of this sinful world, with minds and hearts purified by following and obeying God’s word, in thought, attitude, and intention—they will embark on a journey of spiritual healing and personal liberation. In doing so, they will overcome the world and mirror the boundless mercy of God toward humanity.
