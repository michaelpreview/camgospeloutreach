Event Recap

Spiritual Renewal & Fellowship

The Arusha Pastors Prayer Retreat was a divinely orchestrated gathering that brought together over 700 pastors from across Tanzania, representing a wide range of denominations and Christian traditions. United by a deep hunger for God's presence, these leaders convened to seek the voice of the Holy Spirit and receive teachings on critical topics for the Church in these times: Endtime Prophecy, Love, Holiness, Forgiveness, and Financial Debt Cancellation. The Word of God was ministered powerfully through His chosen vessels—Evangelists Joseph Wanyama, John Begisen, and Kakai Kuteli—whose anointed messages stirred hearts and strengthened faith. Worship sessions, filled with the tangible presence of God, were passionately led by local praise teams and the vibrant CAM Ministries Choir, creating an atmosphere of deep reverence, joy, and spiritual breakthrough.

Impact

The retreat became not just a conference, but a sacred encounter, leaving the pastors refreshed, empowered, and ignited for greater Kingdom impact. The impact was far-reaching, touching the lives of the participants who left with renewed purpose and strengthened faith. Many reported experiencing personal breakthroughs, healing, and a deeper connection with God. The event fostered a sense of belonging and support that continues to resonate beyond the retreat itself, inspiring ongoing spiritual growth and ministry outreach in the region.
